Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SportsAmigo

How do I register for an event?

+

To register for an event, navigate to the specific event page (Basketball, Cricket, or Football), find the event you're interested in, and click the "Register Now" button. Follow the prompts to complete your registration. You'll need to be logged in as a Player or Manager to register.

How can I create my own team?

+

To create a team, log in as a Manager, navigate to your dashboard, and select "Create New Team." You'll need to provide team details, including name, sport, logo, and other relevant information. Once created, you can invite players to join your team.

Can I organize my own tournament?

+

Yes! Users registered as Organizers can create and manage tournaments. Log in as an Organizer, go to your dashboard, and select "Create New Event." You'll be guided through the process of setting up dates, venues, rules, and registration details.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets. All payments are secured with industry-standard encryption for your safety.

How can I contact sport-specific support?

+

For sport-specific inquiries, you can contact our dedicated support teams via the email addresses listed in the Sports-Specific Support section on this page. Alternatively, use the contact form and select the relevant sport from the dropdown menu.
