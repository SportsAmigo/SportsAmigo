Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for events?

+

To register for events, log into your SportsAmigo account and navigate to the "Events" page. Select the event you want to participate in and click the "Register" button. Follow the prompts to complete your registration. You'll receive a confirmation email once your registration is successful.

How do I create a team?

+

To create a team, go to your profile dashboard and select "My Teams" from the menu. Click on "Create New Team" and fill out the required information including team name, sport, and description. You can then invite members to join your team using their email addresses or SportsAmigo usernames.

Can I organize my own tournament?

+

Yes! SportsAmigo allows users to organize their own tournaments. Go to the "Tournaments" section, click "Host Tournament," and follow the steps to set up your event. You'll need to provide details such as date, location, format, and registration requirements. Our team will review your submission and help you get started.

How do I contact support for a specific sport?

+

For sport-specific support, you can email us at support@sportsamigo.com with the sport name in the subject line. Alternatively, you can use the contact form on this page and select the specific sport from the dropdown menu. Our specialized team members will respond to your inquiry within 24 hours.
