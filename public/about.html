About Us

Learn more about SportsAmigo and our mission to connect sports enthusiasts

Our Story

SportsAmigo was founded in 2025 by a group of sports enthusiasts who saw a gap in how sports events were organized and managed in India. What began as a small project to help local basketball tournaments has now grown into a comprehensive platform supporting multiple sports across the country.

The idea was simple: create a platform that makes it easier for players to find events, for managers to organize teams, and for event organizers to run successful tournaments. Today, SportsAmigo serves thousands of sports enthusiasts, connecting players, teams, and organizers in ways that weren't possible before.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To democratize sports management and make sports more accessible to everyone by providing innovative digital solutions that connect players, teams, and organizers.

At SportsAmigo, we believe that organized sports should be accessible to anyone with a passion to play, regardless of their background or resources. Our platform removes traditional barriers by providing:

  • Simplified event discovery and registration for players
  • Streamlined team management tools for coaches and managers
  • Comprehensive event organization capabilities for tournament hosts
  • Community-building features to connect sports enthusiasts
  • Inclusive participation opportunities for athletes of all skill levels

Through technology and innovation, we aim to transform the landscape of amateur and semi-professional sports in India, creating a more vibrant, connected, and accessible sporting ecosystem for generations to come.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity
