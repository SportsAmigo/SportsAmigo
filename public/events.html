City 3x3 Basketball Challenge

April 15, 2023 Central Arena, Downtown 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Event Description

The City 3x3 Basketball Challenge is the premier street basketball tournament in the region, bringing together teams of all skill levels to compete in a fast-paced, action-packed day of hoops.

Tournament Format

  • Team Size: 3 players + 1 substitute
  • Divisions: Men's Open, Women's Open, U18, U16
  • Format: Group stage followed by knockout rounds
  • Game Duration: 10 minutes or first to 21 points

Prizes

  • 1st Place: $1,000 + Championship Trophy
  • 2nd Place: $500
  • 3rd Place: $250
  • MVP Award: Custom jersey and basketball

Registration Details

  • Entry Fee: $120 per team
  • Registration Deadline: April 10, 2023
  • Contact: basketball@sportsamigo.com

T20 City Championship

April 20-30, 2023 Central Cricket Ground Multiple matches daily

Event Description

The T20 City Championship is the most prestigious cricket tournament in the city, featuring 16 teams competing in the exciting T20 format. Witness thrilling matches, spectacular batting, and fierce bowling as teams battle for the championship title.

Tournament Format

  • Team Size: 11 players + 4 substitutes
  • Format: 4 groups of 4 teams, followed by quarterfinals, semifinals, and final
  • Match Duration: 20 overs per side
  • Tournament Duration: 10 days

Prizes

  • Champion Team: $3,000 + Trophy
  • Runner-up: $1,500
  • Best Batsman: $500 + Medal
  • Best Bowler: $500 + Medal
  • Player of the Tournament: $1,000 + Medal

Registration Details

  • Entry Fee: $300 per team
  • Registration Deadline: April 10, 2023
  • Contact: cricket@sportsamigo.com

City 5-a-side Football Cup

April 25-26, 2023 Downtown Football Arena 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Event Description

The City 5-a-side Football Cup is a high-energy, fast-paced football tournament played on smaller pitches with smaller goals. This exciting format emphasizes skill, quick thinking, and teamwork. Join us for a weekend of non-stop football action!

Tournament Format

  • Team Size: 5 players + 3 substitutes
  • Divisions: Open, Corporate, U21, Veterans (35+)
  • Format: Group stage followed by knockout rounds
  • Game Duration: 20 minutes (10 minutes per half)

Prizes

  • 1st Place: $2,000 + Championship Trophy
  • 2nd Place: $1,000
  • 3rd Place: $500
  • Golden Boot: Custom trophy for top scorer
  • Best Goalkeeper: Custom trophy

Registration Details

  • Entry Fee: $200 per team
  • Registration Deadline: April 20, 2023
  • Contact: football@sportsamigo.com
